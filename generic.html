Restaurant App
Browse & Order online

My restaurant app offers a seamless dining experience, allowing users to browse menus, customize orders, and skip the lines with ease. Leveraging SwiftUI, Xcode, and Core Data, I've crafted a user-friendly platform that communicates with the restaurant API to provide real-time menu updates. Whether you're craving a burger with extra cheese or a salad with specific toppings, this app empowers you to personalize your order effortlessly. With streamlined navigation and accurate menu options, dining convenience has never been easier. Say hello to hassle-free online ordering and embrace a new era of culinary exploration at your fingertips.
